Our Story

From Nigerian Soil to Your Table

Founded in 1981 and headquartered in Sango-Otta, Ogun State, Erisco Foods Limited is Nigeria's most trusted indigenous food manufacturer — with a production capacity of 450,000 metric tonnes annually and 2,500 hectares of cultivated Nigerian farmland.

We're not just a food company. We're a statement that Nigeria can feed itself, export to the world, and be proud of every product that bears our name. From our tomato paste to our seasoning cubes, every product is a promise: quality you can trust, grown and made right here at home.

Farm-to-Table Integrity

We own our 2,500-hectare farm. That means we control quality from seed to shelf — no middlemen, no compromise.

Certified & Trusted

Every product is NAFDAC-certified and SON-approved. When you see the Erisco name, quality is guaranteed.

Built for Nigeria

Affordable prices, authentic flavours, and 3,000+ Nigerians employed. We grow when Nigeria grows.
