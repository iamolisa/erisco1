Who We Are

Nigeria's Most Trusted Food Manufacturer

Erisco Foods Limited is the leading indigenous manufacturer of food products in Nigeria. Incorporated in 1981 and headquartered at KM 45 Lagos-Abeokuta Expressway, Sango-Otta, Ogun State, we have spent over four decades building something rare: a fully Nigerian food business that doesn't just compete with imports — it surpasses them.

Our 450,000 metric tonne annual production capacity comes from a fully integrated supply chain: 2,500 hectares of cultivated farmland, state-of-the-art processing facilities, and a workforce of over 3,000 committed Nigerians. Every product we make — from our flagship tomato paste to our Erisco Tea range — carries NAFDAC certification and SON approval.

Our Mission

To produce the best quality and most affordable food products for Nigerian consumers, while building a supply chain that keeps value within Nigeria.

Our Vision

To be the undisputed #1 food manufacturer in Africa — not by acquiring imports, but by growing, processing, and distributing Nigeria's own produce to the world.

Our Values

Quality without compromise. Affordability without sacrifice. Pride in every product that carries the Erisco name — whether it goes to a Lagos market or an export container.
