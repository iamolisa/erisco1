The Flagship

Erisco

Nigeria's #1 Tomato Paste Brand

Erisco is the name that put Nigeria on the map as a food producer. Since 1981, the Erisco brand has been synonymous with rich, thick, affordable tomato paste — the kind that's been a cornerstone of Nigerian cooking for generations.

Today the Erisco brand extends beyond tomato paste into premium teas and beverages — same commitment to quality, same accessible price, always proudly Nigerian. Whether it's the 70g pack in a market stall or the 2.5kg catering tin in a restaurant kitchen, Erisco delivers consistency you can stake your reputation on.

Dairy & Beverages

Nagiko

Nourishment for Every Nigerian Family

Nagiko was born from a simple insight: Nigerian families deserve nutritious dairy products that don't stretch the budget. Every Nagiko product — from fat-filled milk powder to chocolate cubes — is formulated to deliver real nutritional value at an accessible price point.

Whether it's the morning cup of Nagiko milk that starts a child's day or the hot chocolate that ends it, Nagiko is about the small, daily rituals of nourishment that make a family feel cared for.

Seasoning & Spice

Ric-Giko

The Soul of Nigerian Cooking

No Nigerian dish is complete without the right seasoning. Ric-Giko seasoning cubes and spice blends are crafted to deliver the authentic depth of flavour that Nigerian cooks demand — not a generic approximation, but a genuine, bold taste that elevates every meal from good to unforgettable.

Available in chicken, beef, and fish variants — plus a versatile herbs & spices blend — Ric-Giko gives every cook, from the home kitchen to the professional catering operation, exactly what they need to produce authentic Nigerian food at its finest.
